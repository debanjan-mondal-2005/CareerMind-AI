Networking & Job Search Strategies

Online Presence:
- LinkedIn: complete profile, relevant skills, posts about projects
- GitHub: active, well-documented repositories
- Kaggle: competition rankings and notebooks

Networking:
- Attend local meetups (AI/ML, Python)
- Join online communities (Reddit r/datascience, Discord groups)
- Reach out for informational interviews

Job Boards:
- LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, Wellfound (startups)
- Company career pages directly

Application Tips:
- Tailor resume to each job description
- Write a short cover letter highlighting specific projects
- Follow up after applying (if possible)

Hidden job market: many roles are filled via referrals.
