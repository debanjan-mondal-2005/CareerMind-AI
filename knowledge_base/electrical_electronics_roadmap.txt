# Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) Roadmap

## Core Specializations
1. Power Systems: Generation, Transmission, Distribution, Smart Grids.
2. Control Systems: Automation, Robotics, PID Controllers.
3. Microelectronics & VLSI: Chip Design, FPGA, Verilog, VHDL.
4. Embedded Systems: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, STM32, RTOS.
5. Signal Processing: DSP, Audio/Video Compression, Wireless Comm.

## Essential Software Tools
- MATLAB/Simulink: Simulation and mathematical modeling.
- LTspice/PSpice: Circuit simulation.
- AutoCAD Electrical: Industrial electrical design.
- Altium Designer/KiCad: PCB Design and Layout.

## Key Hardware Skills
- Soldering and Circuit Prototyping.
- Using Oscilloscopes, Multimeters, and Spectrum Analyzers.
- PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers) for Industrial Automation.

## Emerging Technologies
- Electric Vehicles (EV): Battery Management Systems (BMS), Motor Controllers.
- Renewable Energy: Solar Inverters, Wind Turbine Control.
- IoT (Internet of Things): Connecting hardware to the cloud.
- Robotics: ROS (Robot Operating System), Kinematics.
