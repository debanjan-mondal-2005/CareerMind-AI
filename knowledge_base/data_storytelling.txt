Data Storytelling & Visualization

Why It Matters:
Insights are useless if they can't be communicated. Storytelling turns data into decisions.

Key Tools:
- Tableau / Power BI (dashboards)
- Python: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly
- Excel (pivot charts still widely used)

Story Structure:
1. Context – business problem
2. Analysis – what you found
3. Conclusion – actionable recommendation
4. Visual – clean chart supporting the story

Best Practices:
- Avoid chart junk
- Use color purposefully
- Label axes and provide titles
- Know your audience
