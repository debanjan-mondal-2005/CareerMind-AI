# Medical, Nursing, and Healthcare Career Guide

## Medical Professions
1. Specialized Doctors: Cardiology, Oncology, Neurology, Surgery.
2. Dentistry: Oral health, Orthodontics.
3. Pharmacy: Drug development, clinical pharmacology.
4. Physiotherapy: Rehabilitation, Sports medicine.

## Nursing Specializations
1. Registered Nurse (RN): General patient care.
2. Critical Care Nurse (ICU): Specialized emergency care.
3. Pediatric Nurse: Healthcare for children.
4. Geriatric Nurse: Healthcare for the elderly.
5. Nurse Practitioner (NP): Advanced clinical roles.

## Essential Healthcare Skills
- Patient Assessment and Vital Monitoring.
- Medical Documentation and Record Keeping (EHR/EMR).
- Emergency Response: CPR, BLS, ACLS.
- Compassion and Communication: Interacting with patients and families.

## Medical Technology (MedTech)
- Diagnostic Imaging: MRI, CT Scans, Ultrasound.
- Telemedicine: Remote consultations.
- AI in Healthcare: Predictive diagnostics, robotic surgery.
- Bioinformatics: Genomic data analysis.

## Career Path in Healthcare
- Entrance Exams: NEET (India), MCAT (USA/Canada), PLAB (UK).
- Licensing: NCLEX for Nursing, Medical Board Certifications.
- Hospital Management and Public Health Administration.
